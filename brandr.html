02 · The Origin · Why it exists

The problem
found me first.

"I didn't start with a theoretical SaaS idea. I was running multiple WatchTheFall pages and kept hitting the same wall: every piece of source content had to be downloaded, branded, captioned, formatted and published — again and again, across page after page. Brandr came from that real workflow pain. I built the product because I was the first user of the problem."

WatchTheFall Media created the problem at scale — many pages, many identities, the same branding work repeated daily. That made it the perfect place to feel the pain, then test the fix. The media network isn't a side project; it's the proving ground the product was born in.

One network · Many page identities · The same repeated branding work

03 · The Product

Brandr turns repeated
creative work into software.

Paste a link, choose a brand identity, render, publish. Four steps — regardless of how many pages you're running.

Step 01
Fetch
Paste a TikTok, Instagram or YouTube link — or upload your own clip.
Step 02
Apply Brand
Select the brand preset. Logo position, watermark, overlay — all saved.
Step 03
Render
Brandr processes and outputs a clean, format-correct file.
Step 04
Publish
Download and post. Your content. Your brand. Everywhere.

Workflow observed under two minutes · live at brandr.online

who post at scale.

Primary use case

Multi-page operators
Running regional pages, niche accounts, or a content network? Brandr was built for this exact workflow — it's what powers the WatchTheFall network across 20+ pages. One tool, every identity, consistent output.
